Privacy Policy

App: BeyDeck – Deck Builder & Meta Tracker
Developer: Santiago Pérez Sánchez
Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

BeyDeck may collect the following data through third-party services:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID) — used for ad personalization and analytics
  • Usage data — app interactions, features used, crash reports
  • Diagnostics — performance data and crash logs

BeyDeck does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information directly.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use your device identifier to show personalized ads.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app.
  • App functionality: Usage data helps us improve the app experience.

3. GDPR – European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we will ask for your consent before processing your data for advertising purposes. You may withdraw consent at any time through the app settings.

If you do not consent, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

4. Children

BeyDeck is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Our app is designed as a reference tool for Beyblade X and does not encourage in-app purchases or data collection from minors.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Your collection and deck data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. Remote data (parts catalog, meta stats) is fetched from Supabase and is not linked to any personal identifier.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most data can be deleted by uninstalling the app. For advertising data, you can reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
